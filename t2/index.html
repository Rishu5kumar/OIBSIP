Biography

Kadar Khan was a legendary Indian film actor, comedian, script and dialogue writer. He was born on December 11, 1937, in Kabul, Afghanistan, and later moved to India. Kadar Khan's contribution to the Indian film industry is unparalleled, and he is remembered for his versatile roles and impeccable comic timing. During his illustrious career, Kadar Khan appeared in over 300 films and wrote dialogues for more than 250 films. His collaborations with actors like Govinda and Shakti Kapoor in the 90s were particularly memorable. He received several awards for his outstanding contribution to Indian cinema. Kadar Khan's impact on the film industry is lasting. His legacy lives on through his timeless performances and memorable dialogues. He continues to be an inspiration for aspiring actors and writers.
